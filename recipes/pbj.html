Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich is a very traditional American sandwich. It's been enjoyed by everyone, from kids at school to adults at work. Hard to beat the savory and sweet flavors.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread. Any bread will work, but typically white or wheat bread is preferred
  • Peanut butter, enough to cover a one side of a slice of bread. Either smooth or chunky will work.
  • Jelly, enough to cover another slice of bread. There's no traditional type of jelly used and people have strong opinions. Grape, Blueberry, and Strawberry Jellies are common.

Steps

  1. Take a slice of bread
  2. On one side, spread the peanut butter so it covers the bread evenly
  3. Take the other slice of bread
  4. Add jelly to one side of the bread, enough that it covers the bread
  5. Put the slices of bread together so that peanut butter and jelly are touching
  6. Enjoy!
